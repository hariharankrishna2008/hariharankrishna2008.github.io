Before you start

You'll need your own Anthropic API key

This tutor calls Claude directly from your browser — there's no server involved, so it runs entirely from this page. On the tutor page, paste your API key into the Settings box once; it's saved only in your browser's local storage and sent straight to Anthropic, never anywhere else. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

Because the key lives in your browser, don't share your deployed link publicly — anyone with it could use your key. Setting a spend limit on the key in your Anthropic console is a good extra safeguard.
